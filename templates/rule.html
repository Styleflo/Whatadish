How to play ?

You will see the image of a dish accompanied by its name.

Your goal is to guess its country of origin by typing your answer. (in English of course !)

Points system:

  • ✅ Correct answer on the first try : +100 points
  • ❌ Wrong answer : the points you earn after each attempt will be decreased

Try to get the best score possible ! Good luck ! 🍽️
